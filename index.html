The Story

Why Married & Outside exists

The Story Behind
Married & Outside

When I got married, I didn't realise how difficult it would be to meet other married couples.

Not because people don't want friendship.

But because life gets busy.

Everyone is working, building families, travelling, moving house and figuring out life. Before you know it, years have passed and you've never really found your people.

Married & Outside started with a simple idea: what if there was an easier way for married couples to meet?

What if friendship didn't have to happen by accident?

What if we intentionally created space for couples to connect, build genuine friendships and feel part of a community?

This event is just the beginning of something intentional.
